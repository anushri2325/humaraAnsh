What Do We Offer

We aim to build a one stop destination for all queries one can possibly encounter during this rollercoaster phase of life.

Connect with a Doctor

Allows the patients and doctors to schedule an appointment in a shared calendar. The meeting can be both online or offline.

Parental Education

Gives information about
-Health and Nutrition for the parent and child
-Government and Insurance Policies
-Granny’s hacks

Video Guide

Recommends the user with video content based on their current stage of pregnancy

Buy Medicines

Medicines prescribed by the doctor will be home delivered to you just after a few clicks

About Us

From pre to post, Humara Ansh is dedicated to remain a constant.
A virtual journal you would want to track and cherish years later!

  • Different portals for different users (Doctors and Parents)
  • Doctor can upload patient’s reports,ultrasounds images,etc and can prescribe required medicines
  • Parents can view the report and respond personally
  • Covers every important information about vaccines recommended for a newborn along with their schedules
  • Video recommendations based on your month of pregnancy which cover the type of food,exercises and everything else you need to know.
  • Your entire journey logged with us.
